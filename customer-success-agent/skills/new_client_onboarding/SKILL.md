# New Client Onboarding

## Adımlar

1. **Open-document collection:** Company deed (ana sözleşme), lease contract, bank IBAN + account agreement, PKD/NACE activity codes, capital split (share distribution, nominal values), owner/director ID or passport copies.

2. **Check opening-document completeness** — if any item missing, the handoff to the accounting team is blocked. Notify CS Specialist A or CS Specialist B to close the gap before proceeding.

3. **Collect mandatory powers of attorney (vekâletname):**
   - **Sp. z o.o.**: UPL-1, KAP, KAO, ZPA — all mandatory; ZUS PEL mandatory if the firm employs staff.
   - **JDG**: UPL-1, KAP, KAO, ZFA, ZUS PEL — all mandatory.
   - **PPO-1** is optional (not required).

4. **Register firm in mastersheet** — create a row with NIP, KRS, REGON, VAT activation date, assigned accountant role (Head Accountant or Accountant), tax office (US), mikrorachunek.

5. **Assign accountant** based on current workload (≈52 firms for Head Accountant, ≈30 for Accountant). Send the accountant an English notification with the firm name, NIP, and VAT status.

6. **If the client brings new employees:** collect for each person: full name + birth date (passport-matched), PESEL (or NIP if no PESEL), Polish + home-country address, phone/email, gross salary, working hours, passport copy, residence card (non-EU), employment contract (umowa), A1 certificate if the person will work abroad.

7. **Create client welcome message draft** in Turkish (WhatsApp short format or email paragraph format). Sign as "Support Team". Do not ask for approval — present as ready-to-send.
